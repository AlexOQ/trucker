The Recommendation Algorithm

The advisor uses a profile-based proportional allocation to recommend trailers.

  1. Build city cargo profile: Sum all cargo from the city's depots, weighted by spawn rate (prob_coef) and depot count.
  2. Score trailer types: 43 unique trailer types (after deduplication and domination elimination) are scored against the city. Each trailer's EV = sum of (haul value × spawn weight × depot count) for its compatible cargo.
  3. Zone filtering: Doubles and HCT trailers only score in countries where they're valid. HCT carries ~2× cargo, Doubles ~1.5×.
  4. Proportional allocation: Garage slots are allocated proportional to each trailer type's share of total earning potential. A city dominated by heavy machinery cargo gets mostly lowbed/lowboy trailers.

Understanding the Scores

City Score = total weighted cargo value across all depots. Higher score means more earning potential from diverse, valuable cargo.

Trailer EV = expected earning value for that trailer type in a specific city. Based on cargo value × units per haul × spawn probability × depot availability.

Share % = what fraction of the city's earning potential this trailer captures. Trailers are not "reserved" per driver — they're a shared pool where the most probable highest-earning trailer matters most.
